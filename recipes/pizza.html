Itallian Special Pizza

Description

A dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients, baked quickly—usually, in a commercial setting, using a wood-fired oven heated to a very high temperature—and served hot

Ingredients

    1/2 cup tomato sauce
    1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    1 ball pizza dough*, stretched for a pizza pie
    1/2 pound fresh mozzarella, sliced into rounds
    1 to 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, freshly grated

The Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. In a bowl combine the tomato sauce and 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes.
  3. Spread the mixture over the stretched pizza dough.
  4. Top with the sliced mozzarella.
  5. Brush the crust with olive oil and sprinkle with the grated Parmesan.
  6. Place the pizza on a baking sheet and bake until the cheese is golden and bubbly, about 10 to 12 minutes.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven. Let cool for 5 minutes.
  8. Slice and serve hot or at room temperature.
